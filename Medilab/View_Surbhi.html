Dr. Amrita Rao

Dr. Surbhi Anand Sharma has more than 20 years of experience in Vascular Interventions and Surgery. His area of interest are: Carotid surgery/Interventions (Stroke prevention clinics) ,Advanced aortic interventions and surgery ,Limb salvage and Prevention of Amputation ,Dialysis access Clinics - creation, maintenance and salvage of dialysis access ,Venous clinics - varicose veins and DVT. He is a life member of the Nepal Medical Association, Surgical Society of Nepal, European Association of Cardiothoracic Surgeons, Nepal Cleft lip and Palate Association, Cancer Society of Nepal, Association of Surgeons of India and Association of Thoracic and Cardiovascular Surgeons of India.
